About Me

Hello! My name is Gemma L. Tubigon, and I am a 20-year-old student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information System (BSIS). As a dedicated student and avid movie lover, I have always found joy and inspiration in the world of cinema. Movies have been a significant part of my life, offering not just entertainment, but also a lens through which I can explore diverse cultures, ideas, and stories.

I created this website because of my deep love for movies and my desire to share my thoughts and insights with others. Through this platform, I aim to delve into the intricate details of filmmaking and foster a community of fellow movie enthusiasts. I believe that movies have the power to connect people and spark meaningful conversations. By sharing my reviews, analyses, and discussions on a wide range of films, I hope to inspire others, provide new perspectives, and create a go-to destination for all things cinematic.
